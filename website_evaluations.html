Website Evaluations

Apple Website

The official Apple website showcases its range of products, services, and innovations in a modern, visually appealing design.

  • Contrast: The site uses high contrast between text and background, making content stand out clearly.
  • Repetition: Fonts, colors, and layout are consistent, reinforcing the brand's identity and providing a unified experience.
  • Alignment: Elements are neatly aligned, ensuring a clean and organized appearance that guides the user's eye naturally.
  • Proximity: Related items are grouped together, and there is ample space between distinct sections, making it easy to navigate.

Arngren Website

Arngren is a Norwegian e-commerce site offering a variety of gadgets, but its design is cluttered and overwhelming, leading to a poor user experience.

  • Contrast: The site lacks sufficient contrast, with text blending into the chaotic background, making it hard to focus.
  • Repetition: There's no cohesive repetition of fonts, colors, or layout, resulting in a jumbled and inconsistent visual experience.
  • Alignment: The layout lacks clear alignment, with elements placed randomly, causing confusion and difficulty in navigation.
  • Proximity: Unrelated items are placed too close together, making it difficult to distinguish between different sections and products.
